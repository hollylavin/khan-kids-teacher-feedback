Teacher Feature Requests

Synthesis of 6 Khan Kids focus-group sessions, including one Spanish-language session.

  • Bars show the number of distinct teachers who raised each feature, on a scale from 0 to 20.
  • Click any row to see the teachers who raised it, the sessions it surfaced in, and representative quotes.

How to read this

Every feature below was surfaced by at least one teacher during a focus-group session. A teacher is counted once per feature even if they returned to the point later in the same session. Explicit agreement in the chat (“same as Amy,” “I agree”) counts as a mention; a thumbs-up or silent nod does not.
