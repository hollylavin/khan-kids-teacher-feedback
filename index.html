How to read this

Every feature below was surfaced by at least one teacher during a focus-group session. A teacher is counted once per feature even if they returned to the point later in the same session. Explicit agreement in the chat (“same as Amy,” “I agree”) counts as a mention; a thumbs-up or silent nod does not.
